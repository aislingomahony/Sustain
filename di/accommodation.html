Accommodation

There is plenty of accommodation in Dublin, often best booked through websites such as www.booking.com or www.trivago.com, in particular we suggest:

Hostels:

Neighbouring the Jameson Distillery, Generator Dublin can be found in the emerging district of Smithfield Square conveniently close to central Dublin. Utilising raw materials like exposed brick and stone, glass, black steel and recycled fixtures, the property boasts an air of uniqueness that only Generator can offer. Choose a shared room, quad or private en-suite, with LED reading lights and Wi-Fi at your fingertips.

3-star Hotels:

O'Callaghan Mont Clare Hotel is located on doorstep of Trinity College is located right in the heart of historical Dublin where, Government buildings and Museums were built on Georgian Squares filled with restaurants, businesses and residences that combine to create our cities unique character and bustle.

Central Hotel in Temple Bar is a short walk from Trinity College Dublin, and has a range of rooms from singles to quadruples, all en-suite.

4-Star Hotels:

O'Callaghan Davenport Hotel can be found on the northwest corner of Merrion Square and the doorstep of Trinity College. The Italianate facade dates back to 1863 and is on the register of historic features of Ireland. The building opened as a four star hotel in 1993 and offers both a classical, traditional elegant experience in Dublin with a superb city centre location. Click here to view current rates or make a booking: Trinity College January 2016 - O'Callaghan Davenport Hotel

 

Self-catering apartments:

Staycity serviced apartments are located on Millenium walk, just across the river from Trinity College. One and two bedroom apartments are available, sleeping up to 3 and 6 people, with well-equipped kitchens and a living area.
